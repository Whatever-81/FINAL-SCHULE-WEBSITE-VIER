Places must visit

Prithvi Cafe

I had visited in December

Quaint little resturant tucked inside by the lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard saying - Prithvi Cafe while you are on the main road. The restuarant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious and the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by two people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. It hosts the famous "prithvi theater fest" which you must attend if possible. :-)



Jantar Mantar: Jaipur

I had visited in February 2019

It's a wonderful place. Must visit this place. Must hire a tour guide so it will be easier to understand everything in it. It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls. It is a historical site famous among tourists. It contains a number of different devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the Indian history. It has a nominal ticket price of 15 rupees for Indians but about 225 - 250 rupees for foreigners. Nice place to visit. We had an incredible experience visiting this observatory and seeing the accuracy of the astronomical instruments built around three-hundred years ago.
